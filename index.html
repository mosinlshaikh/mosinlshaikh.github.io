? COMMERCIAL FAQ

Important answers before you enquire.

How much will my project cost?

Cost depends on confirmed scope, users, features, integrations, content, security, testing and delivery requirements. Share a short requirement first; a quotation follows discovery and clarification.

How long will delivery take?

A realistic schedule is proposed after requirements and dependencies are reviewed. Content readiness, approvals, integrations and changes can affect delivery.

Will I receive source code and ownership?

Source-code handover, intellectual-property rights, licensed components and deployment access are defined in the written proposal or agreement for each project.

Do you keep client projects confidential?

Yes. Private client identities, systems, credentials, data and commercial information are not published without explicit authorisation. An NDA can be considered when required.

Are domain, hosting and third-party services included?

They may be included or billed separately depending on scope. Domain, hosting, APIs, payment gateways, licences, messaging, cloud and app-store charges are identified before agreement.

What happens after delivery?

Deployment, handover, training, maintenance, monitoring and future improvements are provided only when included in the agreed scope or a separate support plan.
